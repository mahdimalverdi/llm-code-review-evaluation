# Threats to Validity

Suggested structure:

## Literature Selection

- The paper pool may be incomplete.
- Recent papers may change quickly.
- Some papers are preprints.

## Coding and Synthesis

- Some categories are inferred from papers rather than directly reported.
- The taxonomy may reflect our interpretation.

## Annotation Study

- Sample size may be small.
- Annotators may disagree on usefulness and actionability.
- Generated comments may depend on model and prompt choices.

## Evaluation and Cost Metrics

- Cost estimates may depend on model provider and implementation.
- Human effort proxies may not generalize across teams.

## Mitigation

- Use explicit coding labels: reported, inferred, our perspective.
- Report annotation agreement.
- Preserve disagreement notes.
- Avoid broad model-superiority claims.
